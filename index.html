About Us

Welcome to our beach hotel in Tok Bali, Pasir Puteh, just a short 50 km away from Kota Baharu. Since its opening in November 2011, our resort has become a favorite destination for visitors from all walks of life. We offer a tranquil escape from the city, allowing guests to immerse themselves in the breathtaking beauty of the beach while reconnecting with their loved ones.

At our beach hotel, we believe in creating memorable moments for our guests. Whether you're seeking relaxation, quality time with your loved ones, or a delectable dining experience, our resort is the perfect choice. Join us and indulge in the tranquility and warmth of our beachside retreat.

Activities

Cycling

Experience the thrill of cycling at our hotel! With well-maintained bicycles and picturesque trails, you can enjoy a refreshing ride while taking in the beauty of nature.

Boating

Embark on a boating adventure at our hotel and discover the wonders of the water! Set sail on our well-equipped boats and cruise along the tranquil waters.

Swimming Pool

Dive into ultimate relaxation and fun at our hotel's swimming pool! Immerse yourself in crystal-clear waters as you escape the heat and enjoy a refreshing swim.

Kayaking

Our kayaking recreation activity offers an exhilarating experience for both beginners and experienced paddlers.

Paintball

Gear up and prepare for an adrenaline-fueled paintball adventure at our hotel! Step into our specially designed battleground and experience the thrill of strategic combat.

Karaoke Room

Unleash your inner superstar at our hotel's karaoke recreation activity! Step into our private karaoke rooms and get ready to sing your heart out.
